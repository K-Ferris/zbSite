About Us

Who are we, What are we about?


We are Zibula Business Intelligence Technologies, the company was incorporated in March of 2017 and formally registered in 08 July 2017, motivated by the lack of development around information communication technology (ICT) in Sub-Saharan Africa and Africa as a continent. We understood that a need to create products or solutions tailor-made for the African market was imperative to efficiently deal with challenges faced by rural communities in Africa. As a continent we face severe financial constraints therefore quality products with a longer life span and adaptability are key to solving and bridging the inequalities faced. Our company is uniquely positioned as it offers the above mentioned solutions and more, while adequately and comprehensively addressing inconsistencies in systems.
