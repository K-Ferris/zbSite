About Us






Who Are We? What are we about?

We are Zibula Business Intelligence Technologies, the company was incorporated in March of 2017 and formally registered in 08 July 2017, motivated by the lack of development around informaion communication technology (ICT) in Sub-Saharan Africa and Africa as a continent. We understood that a need to create products or solutions tailor-made for the African market was imperative to efficiently deal with challenges faced by rural communities in Africa. As a continent we face severe financial constraints therefore quality products with a longer life span and adaptability are key to solving and bridging the inequalities faced. Our company is uniquely positioned as it offers the abover mentioned solutions and more, while adequately and comprehensively addressing inconsistencies in systems.



Vision
To cultivate and promote ICT innovation, development and integration in education and health in Africa through our products and services.

Mission
To develop easy to use, user friendlly, adaptable products that operate efficiently and tailored for the consumer/market to enhance performance and unlock maximum benefits.

Values, Objectives
and Goal
At Zibula we promote innovation, creativity, intelligence, people-orientation, teamwork without sacrificing individualism.
